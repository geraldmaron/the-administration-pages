Reporting objectionable AI-generated content

The Administration uses AI to generate scenarios, advisor analyses, news headlines, and outcomes. We apply automated safety filters, but generated text may occasionally be unexpected or in poor taste.

To report content:

  • Open the outcome dispatch (the news-report screen shown after every decision)
  • Tap Report this content at the bottom
  • Choose a category, optionally add a note, and tap Submit report

Reports go directly to our review queue and are used to improve the safety filters. We commit to acting on flagged content within 24 hours.

Common questions

Is this game about real politics?

No. The Administration is a work of political fiction. Real country names and institution names are used to give scenarios verisimilitude — but every scenario, character, advisor, and outcome is generated by AI and is fictional. Nothing in the game is a statement about any real government, official, or party. See our Terms of Service for the full notice.

How do I save my game?

Go to the System tab → Save Game. Enter a name and tap Save. Saves are stored locally on your device. To load a save, go to System → Load Save.

How do I restart?

System tab → Restart Administration. This ends your current run and returns you to the main menu. Your saved games are not affected.

The app crashed / I lost progress.

Sorry. If the app crashed, a diagnostic crash report was sent automatically (no personal data — see Privacy Policy). If you can reproduce the crash, please email us with: your iOS version, device model, and a description of what you were doing.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. Open the App Store, tap your profile, then Purchase History → find the purchase → Report a Problem.

Why does the AI sometimes say something weird, repetitive, or wrong?

The game uses large language models to generate content on the fly. They occasionally produce content that is off-tone, factually wrong about the real world, or repetitive. The game is for entertainment, not analysis — please don't take in-game claims as factual statements about real countries or events. If something is genuinely offensive (not just odd), use the Report button.

How do I delete my data?

Uninstalling the app removes all local saves and disassociates your device from its anonymous Firebase user ID. We do not store personally identifying information, so there is nothing else to delete on our side. See our Privacy Policy for details.
